FAQ

What should I wear?

Each event has a specific dress code listed. Thursday is cocktail attire, Friday is colorful Indian attire, and Saturday is formal/black tie.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but this is an adults-only celebration. We hope you understand and take this as an opportunity for a weekend getaway!

Can I bring a plus one?

If you've been allocated a plus one, their name will appear on your invitation. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by March 15, 2025. This helps us finalize catering and seating arrangements.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We'll have vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. Please note any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

Is there parking available?

Most venues have nearby parking garages. However, we highly recommend using rideshare services or the subway to avoid parking hassles in NYC.
